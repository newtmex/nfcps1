About NFCPS

NFCPS is short for National Fellowship of Christian Pharmacy Students

NFCPS is a christian body which embodies all pharmacy students in 17 tertiary institutions across Nigeria. We are a body of united pharmacy students spiritually, academically, professionally, morally and socially. NFCPS was set up on 29th March 1990 at OAU Ile-ife with representatives from all pharmacy schools in Nigeria to bring pharmacy students together.

Our Values

Mission and Vision:

In order to achieve our mission to pharmacy students and the community, we organize:

  • Community Outreach
  • Career Seminars
  • Academic Seminars
  • Prison Outreach
  • Hospital Outreah
  • Zonal Pharmaceutical Outreaches
  • Magazine Production and Continuous Publication
  • National Convention

Aims and Objectives

  • To build members into spiritual maturity, responsible Christian Pharmacist who would provide true professional and selfless service to humanity
  • To promote friendship, corporation, unity and serve as a forum for addressing a wide range of issues with Pharmaceutical and Spiritual implications
  • To promote and encourage the spread of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ
  • To encourage hard work and excellence among members

National Convention

25th to 29th of May, 2016 all christian pharmacy students will be converging at University of Nigeria, Nsukka for the annual National Convention of NFCPS. This will be the 25th of it's kind. It will run with the theme "Rooted Firmly in Christ", Colossians 2:7.
